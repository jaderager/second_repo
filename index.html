Top Ten reasons to live in a van with your partner

  1. No rent!
  2. If you don't like your yard, you can move!
  3. You have all of your stuff with you all the time.
  4. You have all of your stuff with you all the time, so you get rid of junk you don't need.
  5. You develop incredible patience because almost everything you do is a process.
  6. You can go anywhere there are roads!
  7. You can sleep inside National Forests... with bears!
  8. It can being trying for your relationship, so if your partner is a jerk, you find out right away and you can dump them!
  9. You meet SO MANY incredible people.
  10. You meet SO MANY cute animals.
